• Vuollerim Natur och Kultur is thrilled to offer you the chance to immerse your senses in this stunning arctic forest trail with its bounty of changing terrain, diverse nature and endless photographic opportunities.
  • Please Note: Your skill level, general physical ability, the season, and the weather can all impact significantly on your experience. Please check accessibility and expected conditions before heading out. Arctic Circle Gate-Way 97 has friendly advice and recent information on our arctic conditions.
  • Length of trail: 12 km variable terrain.
  • Time frame: 6 hrs – 2days. We suggest at least make a day of it. Its possible to enjoy an over night stay and take in the northern nights (over night equipment is required).
  • What do you need: Fluid. Appropriate footwear & clothing for the season, prevailing & expected weather conditions. A camera & remember your trail map.
  • What may be useful: A better camera, mobile phone, food, sun protection, sunglasses, insect repellent &/or protection, extra &/or alternative clothing, toilet paper, knife, Compass or GPS, matches or lighter, binoculars, cooking items, appropriate winter gear.
  • For your safety: Be aware the Arctic weather can change dramatically. Temperatures may vary between +30 to -40 depending on the season. Refrain from consuming anything you are not absolutely sure of for e.g. mushrooms. Simply take a photo. Avoid drinking still, slow flowing, or warm, water. Emergency number is ‘112’, & take a first aid kit.
  • Regulations: Allemansrätten, hunting, firearms, fishing, access and other regulations exist. If unsure simply enjoy nature, leave nothing but your footprints and take away good memories and great photos. You are now in Laplands Serri Nature Reserve so make time to read the information boards or visit www.lansstyrelsen.se. Thank you for visiting Lapland!
