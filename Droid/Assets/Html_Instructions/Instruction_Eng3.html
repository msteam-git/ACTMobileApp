Directions to get to the start of the trail:
This is where to start and finish your hike along the Arctic Circle Trail and where you begin one of your Arctic adventures.
Parking is available at this east entrance to Serri Nature Reserve.
The trail is marked by the red rings painted on trees.
 
Your starting point coordinates are:
N66 34.690 E20 20.974
 
The trail is located 23.5 km, about 20min, from Vuollerim.
 
How to get there:
From Vuollerim 6000 building, turn left into Murjeksvägen
in 600 m turn left
in 1.1 km turn right
in 5.8 km turn right
in 7.2 km turn left
in 1.4 km turn right
in 7.4 km car park on left.


Vuollerim Natur och Kultur is thrilled to offer you the chance to immerse your senses in this stunning arctic forest trail with its bounty of changing terrain, diverse nature and endless photographic opportunities.

Please Note: Your skill level, general physical ability, the season, and the weather can all impact significantly on your experience. Please check accessibility and expected conditions before heading out. Arctic Circle Gateway 97 has friendly advice and recent information on our arctic conditions.

  • Length of trail: 12 km variable terrain.

  • Time frame: 6 hrs – 2 days. We suggest at least make a day of it. It's possible to enjoy an overnight stay and take in the northern nights (overnight equipment is required).

  • What do you need: Fluid. Appropriate footwear & clothing for the season, prevailing & expected weather conditions. A camera & remember your trail map.

  • What may be useful: A better camera, mobile phone, food, sun protection, sunglasses, insect repellent &/or protection, extra &/or alternative clothing, toilet paper, knife, Compass or GPS, matches or lighter, binoculars, cooking items, appropriate winter gear.

  • What is available to you: water, shelter, prepared wood, fireplace, axe, toilet, various berries (summer & autumn), mushrooms (warning, see below).

  • For your safety: Be aware the Arctic weather can change dramatically. Temperatures may vary between +30 to -40 depending on the season. Refrain from consuming anything you are not absolutely sure of for e.g. mushrooms. Simply take a photo. Avoid drinking still, slow flowing, or warm, water. Emergency number is ‘112’, & take a first aid kit.

  • Regulations: Allemansrätten, hunting, firearms, fishing, access and other regulations exist. If unsure simply enjoy nature, leave nothing but your footprints and take away good memories and great photos.

You are in Laplands Serri Nature Reserve so please make time to read the information boards, or visit www.lansstyrelsen.se.
